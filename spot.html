The SPOT System

Self Positioning Orbital Technology

The world is in need of reliable technology that works in Earth's orbit. That's why I'm working on the SPOT System.

The SPOT Hive: A mobile port for storage and charging of SPOT Bees.

The SPOT Bee: A first-of-its-kind autonomous drone, capable of repairing satellites, providing data, and adjusting orbiting objects.

  • The SPOT Bee is general-use. It is a drone platform capable of any assignment, with opportunities for modification to preform specialized tasks.
  • The SPOT Bee is autonomous. It doesn't require a connection to Earth to complete its assignment.
  • The SPOT Bee is cheap. Due to its longlasting nature and small size, repairing a satellite with the Bee is far cheaper than replacing it.

Current Status: Conceptual design. Prototyping and testing planned.
